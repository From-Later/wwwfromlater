[ Work ]

We drive growth and build resilience. Employing the tools and methods from design thinking and strategic foresight, we empower decision-makers to navigate uncertainty and capitalize on change.

Our tools and materials augment your organization with new levels of sensory capability, agility, and coordination. By accelerating a shared understanding of what is happening in the present, we equip leadership teams to make decisions and plans for the future with greater speed and certainty.

While our strategic foresight practices inherently monitor change, identify threats, prevent surprise, and minimize risk, our projects are designed to help you determine where to allocate capital and human capital in pursuit of new revenue streams and increased profitability.

[ Process ]

Radar—surveying the extreme present, identifying signals of change that could shape the future

Studio—drafting the futures, imagining possibilities and provoking new thinking

Strategy—determining strategic pathways that hold up across a range of possible futures

[ Offerings ]

Strategic Foresight for Long-Term Growth & Resilience

We apply the tools and methods of strategic foresight to help your organization navigate uncertainty and capitalize on change. Our continuous scanning practice extends the reaches of your strategic radar and jumpstarts our process of determining new opportunities for innovation, growth, transformation, and value creation.

Strategic Foresight Training and Capability Building

We tailor our proprietary foresight curriculum to the needs of your organization. Our customized training programs build foresight capacity and foster a forward-looking culture relevant to your business. Over a series of sessions, participants learn the core foundations of strategic foresight through the hands-on experience of applying its tools and methods to their own roles and strategic objectives.

Strategic Foresight for Product, Service, and Brand Innovation

We develop strategies that are empathetic to human needs, economically viable, sensitive to the cultural and political climate, while being operationally viable. Working closely with stakeholders and experts, we prototype and develop concepts that create long-term economic value and differentiation.
